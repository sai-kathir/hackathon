# 🌟 PathVeda – AI Powered Career Guidance Website

## 📌 Problem Statement
Many students feel confused after receiving their exam results because they do not know which colleges, courses, or degrees are best for them. Students often spend hours searching online and still struggle to find the right career path according to their marks, interests, and location.  

PathVeda solves this problem by using Artificial Intelligence to guide students toward suitable colleges and degree options based on their academic performance and personal interests.

---

# 🚀 About PathVeda
PathVeda is an AI-powered career guidance website designed to help students discover the best colleges and degree opportunities. The website provides personalized recommendations by analyzing student details such as marks, interests, and preferred location.

The platform makes career guidance simple, fast, and accessible for students.

---

# 🖥️ Website Flow

## 🔐 Login Page
The website starts with a login page where the user enters:
- Student Name
- School Name

After entering the details, the student proceeds to the next page.

---

## 📍 Location Selection
On the second page, students select their preferred location or area where they want to study.

---

## 📊 Academic & Interest Details
Students then:
- Enter their marks
- Mention their interests or career preferences

Example:
- Engineering
- Medical
- Business
- Arts
- Computer Science

---

# 🤖 What AI Does
The AI system analyzes:
- Student marks
- Career interests
- Preferred location

Based on this information, the AI:
- Suggests top colleges
- Recommends suitable degree programs
- Helps students understand career opportunities
- Provides smarter and personalized guidance

The AI makes the decision process easier and helps students choose better career paths confidently.
